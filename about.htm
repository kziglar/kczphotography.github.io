My photography journey

I have always had a love for photography. Not just the art of a photo, but more so the meaning and impact they serve. Photos give back life to the moments we truly don't ever want to forget. One look at it and it brings back the emotion, joy, and love that was there when the photo was taken. It is the only way we can really freeze time just for a second to remember it forever. I fell in love with the art of taking photos more recently. I bought my first camera in 2020 where I mostly took photos of nature. Hence the "nature-loving". Since then, I have explored all different kinds of photography and have completely fallen in love. I now do all styles of photography but I love to incorporate the beauty of nature when and wherever I can. Let me share my passion for art and photography with you! I promise I will do everything in my power to make sure you have a memorable experience so your raw happiness shines through in those photos! Now that you know some about me, I would like to get to know you!
